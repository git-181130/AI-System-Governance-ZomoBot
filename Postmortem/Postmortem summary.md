# Postmortem Review

## Organisational Learning and Accountability Summary

---

### 1. Overview

This folder contains the frameworks and review records governing post-incident organisational learning within ZomoBot’s governance system.

It documents how incidents are analysed at the organisational level to assess control effectiveness, decision quality, and systemic resilience.

Postmortems represent the formal mechanism for institutionalising lessons learned.

---

### 2. Governance Objectives

The postmortem program is designed to:

* Capture cross-functional learning
* Strengthen governance maturity
* Improve decision-making processes
* Reinforce accountability
* Reduce recurrence risk

Postmortems convert operational failures into strategic improvements.

---

### 3. Scope of Application

Postmortem reviews apply to:

* All critical and high-severity incidents
* Recurrent medium-severity failures
* Significant trust or compliance events
* Regulatory-triggered investigations

Material incidents require formal postmortem documentation.

---

### 4. Review Methodology

ZomoBot’s postmortem framework includes:

* Structured review sessions
* Evidence-based assessment
* Stakeholder interviews
* Governance performance evaluation
* Action planning workshops

This approach ensures balanced analysis.

---

### 5. Accountability and Learning

Postmortems examine:

* Decision pathways
* Control effectiveness
* Communication quality
* Resource allocation
* Organisational pressures

Findings are framed constructively.

---

### 6. Governance Integration

Postmortem outputs feed into:

* Continuous improvement programs
* Policy updates
* EvalOps refinements
* Risk register revisions
* Training initiatives

Learning is operationalised.

---

### 7. Contents

Primary documents include:

* postmortem_framework.md
* Postmortem reports
* Action registers
* Verification records

---

### 8. Review and Maintenance

Postmortem processes are reviewed:

* Quarterly
* After major incidents
* Following regulatory updates

Facilitation standards ensure quality.

---

### 9. Role in Governance Lifecycle

Postmortems represent the primary organisational learning mechanism within the governance lifecycle.

They ensure that system failures lead to lasting institutional resilience.
