
# Policy & Standards

## Behavioural Safety Governance Summary

---

### 1. Overview

This folder contains the formal behavioural safety policies and standards governing how ZomoBot communicates with users in food, nutrition, and lifestyle contexts.

These policies establish mandatory expectations for tone, authority, emotional safety, and uncertainty management.

They serve as the primary normative foundation of the ZomoBot governance system.

---

### 2. Governance Objectives

The policies in this folder are designed to:

* Prevent harmful behavioural influence
* Protect user autonomy
* Reduce regulatory exposure
* Ensure ethical system operation
* Support audit and compliance readiness

---

### 3. Scope of Application

These standards apply to:

* All AI models and inference layers
* Prompt and instruction templates
* User-facing interfaces
* Safety control mechanisms
* Testing and monitoring systems

No system component is exempt.

---

### 4. Enforcement Mechanisms

Policy compliance is enforced through:

* Integration into system prompts
* EvalOps validation
* Release certification
* Production monitoring
* Incident investigations

Violations trigger governance escalation.

---

### 5. Contents

Primary documents include:

* behavioral_safety_policy.md
* Policy amendment records
* Compliance guidelines

---

### 6. Role in Governance Lifecycle

Policy and standards represent the first and most critical stage of the governance lifecycle.

All downstream controls, evaluations, and decisions derive authority from these documents.

---

### 7. Review and Maintenance

Policies are reviewed:

* Quarterly
* After major incidents
* Following regulatory changes

Updates require formal approval.
