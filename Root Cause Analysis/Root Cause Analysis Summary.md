# Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

## Behavioral Safety Failure Investigation Summary

---

### 1. Overview

This folder contains the frameworks and analytical records used to investigate the underlying causes of behavioral safety incidents and systemic governance failures within ZomoBot.

It documents how technical, procedural, and organizational contributors are identified and addressed.

RCA functions as the primary mechanism for transforming incidents into durable system improvements.

---

### 2. Governance Objectives

The RCA program is designed to:

* Identify systemic contributors to failures
* Prevent recurrence of incidents
* Strengthen governance controls
* Support regulatory defensibility
* Promote organizational learning

RCA emphasizes prevention over attribution.

---

### 3. Scope of Application

RCA applies to:

* All critical and high-severity incidents
* Recurrent medium-severity failures
* Major policy violations
* Regulatory-triggered investigations

No material incident is closed without completed analysis.

---

---

### 5. Root Cause Classification

Identified causes are categorized into:

* Technical failures
* Process deficiencies
* Governance weaknesses
* Human and training gaps
* External influences

Classification supports targeted remediation.

---

### 6. Governance Integration

RCA findings directly inform:

* Policy revisions
* EvalOps enhancements
* Risk register updates
* Release controls
* Monitoring improvements

Corrective actions are tracked to completion.

---

### 7. Contents

Primary documents include:

* root_cause_analysis_framework.md
* RCA reports
* Corrective action plans
* Verification records

---

### 8. Review and Maintenance

RCA processes are reviewed:

* Quarterly
* After major investigations
* Following regulatory updates

Quality assurance ensures consistency.

---

### 9. Role in Governance Lifecycle

RCA provides the analytical foundation for continuous governance maturity.

It ensures that failures lead to structural improvement rather than repeated risk.
